Favorite movie

One of my ultimate favorite movie of all times is Hachi: A dogs tale. This movie is about a loyal dog that would accompany its owner to work every day and would continue waiting outside (10 years) at the owners job waiting for him to get out not knowing the owner died. It was one of the movies that got me emotional and everytime I come across that type of breed in real life I get reminded of it. Here is the link see the movie plot.
